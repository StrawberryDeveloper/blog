About Me

Hi, I’m Gabriel Knab! I’m passionate about programming, Unity game development, microcontrollers, and 3D printing. Ever since I can remember, I’ve been fascinated by creating things. And software is incredible because it lets you build anything imaginable.

What I Do

I’m always exploring new technologies and experimenting with ideas, whether it’s a hobby project or learning something entirely new. I love diving into challenges and figuring out how things work, from game mechanics to microcontroller setups.

Hobbies

When I’m not coding or tinkering, you’ll probably find me doing sports like judo, reading a great book, or building cool projects just for fun.

Let’s Build Something

I believe anyone can bring their ideas to life. Whether it’s a game, a gadget, or something completely different, start small and keep building. You’ll be amazed at what you can create.

Thanks for visiting my blog, and I hope you find something here that inspires you to make something of your own!
